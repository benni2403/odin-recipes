Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Parmesan cheese. Fry pancetta in a pan until crispy, then remove from heat and combine with the cooked spaghetti. Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, letting the heat from the pasta cook the eggs to create a creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti
  2. Fry pancetta
  3. Mix eggs and cheese
  4. Combine all ingredients
