Caesar Salad

Description

Chop romaine lettuce and place in a large bowl. Prepare the dressing by mixing mayonnaise, garlic, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and Worcestershire sauce. Toss the lettuce with the dressing, croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese. Serve immediately for a fresh, crunchy salad.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Dijon mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Chop lettuce
  2. Prepare dressing
  3. Toss salad with dressing
  4. Add croutons and cheese
