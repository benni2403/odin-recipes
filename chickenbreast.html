Chicken breast

Description

Slice it up into finger thick slices. Then, put it into a container and season it with paprika powder, garlic, salt and pepper.

In the mean time, warm up your pan and add a little bit of butter. When the butter starts bubbling, add your chicken. Roast evenly and when there are no more pink spots left on the inside, the chicken is ready to be served.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Seasons: Garlic, paprika powder, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Slice up chicken
  2. Season it
  3. Heat up the pan and fry it
